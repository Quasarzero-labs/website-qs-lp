[01 Case Studies]

Concrete work rooted in real business environments.

Energy Anomaly detection

Detect failures before they happen, at industrial scale.

Designed a system able to detect early failure signals automatically across dozens of machines, hundreds of sensors and several years of industrial data.

Impact

A full pipeline from detection to decision, with no gap between data and field operations.

  • Field engineers validate detected anomalies in an interface built for them, without handling raw data.
  • Every validation directly feeds model training, allowing the system to learn continuously.
  • Expert knowledge, previously tacit and scattered, is captured in a structured and durable knowledge base.
Source TotalEnergies Digital Factory Medium article
Automotive Residual values

Predicting the future value of premium and electric vehicles automatically.

Replaced a long, manual process with an automated system that continuously produces future value estimates for premium and battery-electric vehicles.

Impact

Pricing grids generated automatically, where everything used to be manual.

  • No more spreadsheets updated by hand: data is collected, structured and analyzed automatically.
  • Models are tested and compared continuously to maintain the highest possible reliability.
  • Pricing, leasing and strategy teams get clear, always up-to-date decision support.
Energy Forecasting & uncertainty

Anticipating oil production with far greater accuracy.

Designed a forecasting system able to produce far more reliable production estimates and account for uncertainty instead of ignoring it.

Result

2.5× more wells forecast correctly, with the success rate rising from 26% to 65%.

  • Forecasts show not only what is likely to happen, but also the range within which it may happen.
  • Major reduction in unpleasant surprises on future production.
  • A tool directly usable by business teams to guide investment decisions.
