USE CASES

From strategy to production,
across industries.

01 Oil & Gas · Industrial Operations

Deploying 100+ ML models for industrial operations

Designed and deployed a large-scale ML platform for a major oil & gas operator — covering predictive maintenance, anomaly detection, and equipment failure forecasting across production sites. Models were integrated into existing SCADA systems and monitored continuously in production.

OUTCOME Reduction in unplanned downtime and a unified model governance framework across 100+ production-grade models.
02 Automotive · Pricing Strategy

Statistical pricing strategy for the automobile sector

Built a statistical pricing engine for an automotive group, combining market elasticity models, competitor price signals, and demand forecasting. The system replaced manual pricing committees with a data-driven recommendation layer, reviewed and validated by commercial teams.

OUTCOME Measurable margin improvement on high-volume models and a repeatable pricing methodology adopted across three regional markets.
03 Logistics · Supply Chain

ML-driven fleet optimization in supply chain

Developed a fleet optimization model for a logistics operator, combining route planning algorithms, demand prediction, and real-time vehicle telemetry. The solution reduced empty mileage, improved on-time delivery rates, and cut fuel costs through dynamic dispatching.

OUTCOME Significant reduction in fleet operating costs and a scheduling system that adapts daily to demand fluctuations without manual intervention.
